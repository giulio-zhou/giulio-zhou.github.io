Hi I'm Giulio.

I'm a final year PhD Student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University advised by Dave Andersen. I work at the intersection of systems and machine learning.

In Summer 2020, I interned with Jacob Devlin on the Google NLP Team, working on developing efficient models for text retrieval.

In Summer 2019, I interned with Martin Maas on the Google Brain Systems team, working on using Transformers to learn storage policies from distributed traces for datacenter storage systems.

In undergrad, I worked on ML model serving with Joey Gonzalez and at Google as a software engineer.
